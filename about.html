About Us

Welcome to Dotech Business Systems

We have been helping businesses with IT services for 10 years!

Dotech Business Systems was incorporated in 2010 and in the relatively short period of time since its inception, it has established itself as a major ICT company and supplier of telecommunications equipment and engineering services in Kenya with a substantial amount of business generated from both the wireline and wireless businesses.

Dotech Business Systems was organized to be responsive to the emerging new technologies in the ICT industry by constantly monitoring new products and services fitted to the needs of the ICT customers, establish new product lines for value added services of the service providers. In so doing, the company has also employed young highly technical, well disciplined, and trained; field tested and experienced manpower to handle all ICT jobs with a professional's touch. The unending cross training among each of the experienced employees has further enriched the company’s readiness to meet the demand of the future and readiness to serve the needs of its valued customers nationwide. Dotech Business Systems in its mission to give customers total satisfaction has employed a staff to support all equipment sold to its customers. It handles the design, consultancy, building and construction of Network Center, installation and commissioning of Microwave Radio & Antennas, Network IT Security, radio Terminals, Multiplexers, Datacom equipment, Inside and Outside Plant cables (Copper/Fiber Optics), Structured Cabling. It has a long record of accomplishments both in private and government entities including telecommunications.

Our mission is to serve the various needs of the growing industry utilizing the state-of-the-art, 21st century technology. Providing efficient professional services and technical support to ensure fast and effective response to the industry requirements in meeting their needs, and maintain a growing pool of highly trained, professional and well experienced employees, providing them with challenge, foreign training, career growth and better emoluments.

We aim to be the nation’s leader and sought after professional agent of ICT equipment in whatever form and substance.

At Dotech, we commit ourselves to provide high quality service and after sales support to achieve utmost customer satisfaction and care. It is our policy to understand and meet the requirements of our customers, be they internal or external, and to ‘DO IT RIGHT THE FIRST TIME, EVERYTIME'. We are committed to consistently satisfying changing customers’ needs with absolute honesty and integrity.
